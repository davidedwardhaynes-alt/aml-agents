I · Authority & Conduct

HKMA refreshes the stored-value facility authorisation framework

Hong Kong  ·  Issued 8 May  ·  Effective Q3 2026

The Hong Kong Monetary Authority published a revised framework yesterday tightening conduct expectations for stored-value facility operators. The most material changes affect customer due diligence at onboarding for non-resident users, the cadence of transaction monitoring threshold reviews, and the independent compliance review schedule.

Most Tier-1 licensees will need to evidence compliance with the new expectations in their next annual return, and a transitional window for second-tier operators runs until 31 December.
